A successful college career goes far beyond what happens inside the classroom. The Brooklyn Education Innovation Network provides you the extracurricular opportunities you need to get the most out of your college career. BE.IN connects you to exciting businesses in the tech + innovation sector and beyond. And we make sure those opportunities are right here in New York’s brightest borough — Brooklyn.

BE.IN can help you:

  • Find an internship.
  • Find a job after you graduate.
  • Develop your leadership skills as part of the BE.IN Leaders Program.
  • Build community through events like College Town Service Days or our Pitch Contests.
