You are part of one of New York’s finest college towns – 60,000+ students and 11,000 faculty members – all right here in Downtown Brooklyn. The urban environment here means that Brooklyn itself serves as the campus for our college town. That is why BE.IN seeks to connect and facilitate relationships throughout Brooklyn to provide the most beneficial environment for higher education professionals as possible.

We do that in the following ways:

  1. BE.IN Meetups and events help to facilitate faculty engagement and discussion.
  2. BE.IN provides community and professional resources to foster a sense of community.
  3. BE.IN facilitates faculty-business relationships, or relationships between faculty of our member institutions and local businesses for intellectual exchange to foster innovation and creativity in Brooklyn.

Meetups

BE.IN has a major focus on connecting academic institutions with industry, the growing innovation economy, and the broader Downtown Brooklyn community. In an effort to develop meaningful connections, BE.IN has developed a series of faculty and staff meetups to develop and strengthen relationships. Meetups are held year-round throughout the Brooklyn Tech Triangle of Downtown Brooklyn, DUMBO, and the Brooklyn Navy Yard.

Academic Collaborations

BE.IN faculty members are committed to academic excellence. They believe in quality teaching, service, and research. BE.IN is committed to assisting faculty in their scholarly endeavors by strengthening their ties to industry, business partners, nonprofit organizations, and government, which can lead to opportunities for funding, research, consulting work, service-learning projects, and more.
