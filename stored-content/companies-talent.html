Brooklyn Tech Triangle Internship Program

If you need interns but don’t have the budget, consider BTTIP.

The Brooklyn Tech Triangle Internship Program (BTTIP) connects students studying to become web developers, programmers, designers, and engineering technologists with internships at tech and media businesses in the Brooklyn Tech Triangle and surrounding areas.

What does my company receive?

  • College-level interns from New York City College of Technology at no cost to your business. Students are paid $15/hour by the City of New York.
  • Internship takes place Monday-Thursday during regular business hours, for a total of 25 hours per week
  • Guidance and support from BE.IN
  • Opportunity to support your employment pipeline

How do I participate?

  • Commit to hosting students on-site from May-August 2016
  • Commit to providing substantial work experience for students
  • Commit to considering interns for potential open positions

Eligibility

  • Company must be located in the Brooklyn Tech Triangle
  • Internships provided should focus on Tech and Media
  • Commitment to hiring local talent

Interested in learning more about BTTIP? Contact BE.IN Program Manager, Ryan McAllister, at rmcallister@downtownbrooklyn.com. Apply today at here.

BTTIP is a collaboration among New York City’s Tech Talent Pipeline, the Brooklyn Education Innovation Network (BE.IN), the Downtown Brooklyn Partnership, and New York City College of Technology.
