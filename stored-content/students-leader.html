A college degree is one part of the recipe for success. Leadership is what can take you to the next level once you graduate. Leaders know the importance of cultivating an openness to explore your own personal values, along with those of others and the wider community, plus a willingness to adapt to change, find creative solutions, and seize relevant opportunities.

What is the BE.IN Leaders Program?

The BE.IN Leaders Program helps you become a leader within our Brooklyn College Town community, and in your life and career once you graduate. Acceptance into the BE.IN Leaders Program allows you to connect and network with other student leaders, industry professionals, and the larger Brooklyn professional community, while focusing on learning about ethical leadership.

Our one-year leadership program:

  • Supports student development through workshops, events, and networking opportunities
  • Provides opportunities to examine how your own talent, passion, and personal values relate to socially responsible leadership
  • Prepares you to be engaged in ethical leadership for the purpose of promoting positive social change
  • Promotes and provides experiences that develop effective leadership skills

Benefits for joining the program include:

  • Developing an awareness of self and what it means to lead
  • Building a network of peers and industry leaders
  • Learning about opportunities to better connect with Brooklyn’s College Town community
  • Personal growth and development
  • Increased opportunities for internships, jobs, graduate school acceptance, and civic engagement opportunities

Become a BE.IN Leader

BE.IN believes that supporting student leadership development is a foundation for students to become engaged community members. Student leadership involves cultivating a frame of mind for exploring self, others, and community. Leaders develop a skill set to adapt to change, craft creative solutions, and seize opportunities that allows them to become productive members of Brooklyn’s College Town community. Getting involved provide students all across BE.IN institutions with the opportunity to connect with each other, industry leaders, nonprofits, and the much larger Brooklyn community while learning about ethical leadership. BE.IN believes that leadership development could lead to internships, jobs, acceptance into graduate school, and an opportunity for students to becoming civically engaged.

The Program

BE.IN has developed a year-long program to support student leadership development. The program focus is to provide student leaders with purposeful workshops, events, and networking opportunities to develop student’s leadership abilities. The program goals are to:

  • Identify, promote, and provide experiences to support the development of competencies associated with effective leadership
  • Provide students with opportunities to examine their personal values, talents, and passions, and explore how they relate to socially responsible leadership
  • Prepare students to be engaged in ethical leadership for the purpose of promoting positive social change

Eligibility

For the inaugural year of the Leadership Development Program participation will be limited to the Executive Council of Student Government Associations or any Student Leadership Councils as defined by member institutions. Following the inaugural year of the program, students interested in leadership development will be allowed to apply through their respective schools Student Affairs/Life offices.
